Welcome

Welcome to About the Future — where imagination meets innovation.

Join us for a one-of-a-kind event that brings together scientists, engineers, thinkers, and dreamers to explore what the world could look like in the year 2040. From artificial intelligence and space exploration to sustainable cities and quantum breakthroughs, this summit is your gateway to tomorrow.

Top 5 Experiences

  • Witness the world's first flying car race demonstration
  • Experience hands-on neural interface technology
  • Attend a live panel with NASA astronauts
  • Get exclusive access to next-gen robotics prototypes
  • Explore the AI Art & Ethics Gallery, featuring work by machines and humans

Keynote Speakers

We're proud to feature three extraordinary speakers shaping the world of tomorrow:

  1. Dr. Lena Armstrong - Chief Strategist, NASA Mars Operations

    A trailblazer in deep space logistics and interplanetary habitats, Dr. Armstrong will share NASA's latest vision for permanent human presence on Mars — and how Earth technology is getting us there faster than we think.

  2. Prof. Emilio Takeda - AI Ethics Chair, University of Kyoto

    Known for his groundbreaking research on the moral rights of synthetic intelligences, Prof. Takeda will challenge our assumptions about machines, humanity, and responsibility in the age of artificial minds.

  3. Maya El-Sayed - Inventor, 17, Futuremaker Award Winner

    At just 17, Maya designed a clean-energy device that powers entire villages using atmospheric data. She'll talk about the next generation of inventors — and how the future belongs to bold young thinkers.
