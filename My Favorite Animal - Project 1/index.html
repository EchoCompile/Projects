The Pekingese

My favorite animal is the pekingese because my dog is a pekingese. I just love how their tails and hair. My love for pekingese dogs started when my uncle showed me his new adopted dog. I was so happy for him and played hours with his dog. That's why I got one myself. Her name is Manu

Quick Facts about the pekingese

  • Origin: Ancient Chinese breed, once kept by royalty.
  • Size: Small, compact body with a flat face and long coat.
  • Temperament: Loyal, stubborn, and often aloof with strangers.
  • Care: Needs regular grooming and prefers calm environments.

Top 3 Reasons You Love it

  1. Loyal Companion:Always by your side and deeply attached to you.
  2. Adorable Personality:Its mix of stubbornness and charm is irresistible.
  3. Cuddly and Cute:Fluffy, small, and perfect for cozy moments.
